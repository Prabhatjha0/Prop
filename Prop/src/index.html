About Prop

PROP is a Media Marketing And Content Management platform providing it's services across the globe to popular individuals, rising groups and established corporations. Major services offered by us are:

  1. ADVERTISEMENT
  2. INFLUENCER MARKETING MANAGEMENT
  3. CONTENT MANAGEMENT AND SUPPORT
  4. BRANDING

Our platform leverages the latest trends in digital media to deliver tailored solutions for every client, whether you're an individual influencer or a global corporation. We believe in the power of creativity and innovation to drive impactful marketing campaigns. WE also manages many individuals and channels across popular digital media platforms. Our team is dedicated to helping clients grow their presence and connect with audiences worldwide. So, Why not join us and be the part of a dynamic team that is shaping the future of media marketing and content management.It is a great opportunity to work here at PROP.
